BLOCKED ON A SIMPLE PROBLEM



I'll be honest and say that for the vast majority of the Kata, every obstacle I encountered, regardless of how easy or difficult, had a simple solution. I would shake my head when I realised how simple the answer actually was. I felt a touch out of my depth and a little lacking in confidence throughout the entirety of the Kata's, but I have since learned not to overthink the solution as per i was overcomplicating simple tasks and putting in too much thought, it was helpful when I broke down each step into smaller pieces rather than attempting to understand the entire test at once. One simple problem that happens to stand out the most was proably during the the capatalize test, all i had to do to pass the test was to add a space in my join & map array (example below) to pass it... Gotta keep an eye out for those cheeky case sensitive errors aswell, the amount of time spent going over my code to see what the problem was just to realise it was because of capitalization.
